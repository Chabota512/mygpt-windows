> "I have 8GB of RAM and am running Ollama locally. I have downloaded three specific models for my Mechatronics assistant: **qwen3.5:0.8b** (Vision), **phi4-mini** (Math/Logic), and **llama3.2:1b** (Writing).

>

### Why this works for your development:

* **RAM Management:** By setting `MAX_LOADED_MODELS=1`, your laptop will "forget" the Vision model as soon as it's done and "load" the Math model. This prevents the **500 Internal Server Error** you saw earlier.
* **Specialized Accuracy:** `phi4-mini` is significantly better at the math you'll face in Mechatronics (like Laplace transforms or PID tuning) than a general model. 
* **The "Agent" Feel:** To the user, it will look like one very smart AI. Under the hood, your React frontend will show different stages: *"Analyzing image..."* $\rightarrow$ *"Calculating physics..."* $\rightarrow$ *"Formatting report..."*
